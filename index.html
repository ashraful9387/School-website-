About Gossaidubi High School

Gossaidubi High School, established in 1974, has been a beacon of knowledge and discipline in the Goalpara region. With a mission to nurture young minds, the school offers quality education, a strong value system, and excellent infrastructure. The institution prides itself on its qualified faculty, holistic approach to education, and commitment to academic excellence.

Admission

Admissions are open for Classes 6 to 10. For details regarding the admission process, documents required, and fee structure, please visit the school office or contact us directly.
